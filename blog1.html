Pest Of Rice



Major pests on rice



1. Yellow stem borer
Order:- Lepidoptera
Family:- Pyralidae


It is Absolute/Monophagus pest of rice. Interesting Fact
Female have black spots in the wings.
The size of the female is greater than male.
Eggs are covered by brown thin hairy structure.
Pupa is present inside the plant.
Symptoms
The damage is caused by caterpillars. Caterpillar first eat internal succulent part of the plant then stays in the pith. ‘Dead Heart’ formation takes place before flowering and after flowering ‘White Ear Head’ develops. Symptom of the caterpillar is always seen above the water level.

How can we manage “Yellow stem borer”?
• Eggs of the stem borer are present near the leaf tip, hence clipping and burning of tips of seedling before transplanting.
• Since Basmati varieties suffer heavy damage. Think before planting Basmati varieties.
Prasitoids (wasps)Trichogramma sp are used to control the eggs of stemborers.
2) Rice hispa (Diclodispa armigera)
• Order:- Coleoptera
• Family:- Chrysomelidae

Interesting fact
Also called spiny beetle.
• It is a small bluish black bettle having numerous short spines on the body.
Creamy white color grubs remain concealed inside the leaf tissue because they are legless.

Symptoms
• The symptoms are similar as leaf minors.
It mines between the epidermal layes of leaf and pupates in leaf mines.
• Transparent blotches on the leaf surface are seen which are caused by larvae.
• Parallel whitish streaks are seen in leaves which are caused by adult.
Attacked plant look membranous, with blisters and blotches.
Management
• Irrigate the field and collect the floated beetles by nets and killed.
• Clip off the leaf tips before transplanting seedlings.
• Chlorpyriphos @ 625 ml spray with 250 liters of water.
Conserve natural predator like Assassain Bug.
3. Rice gundhi bug/ Rice earhead bug (Leptocorisa acuta)
Order:- Hemiptera
Family:- Coreidae


It is the Periodic pest of rice. Also known as Rice Stink Bug. Interesting fact

Triangular shape head is present.
Stink glands are presend on the either side of the abdomen.
Pest emits pungent smell, therefore named ‘gundhi bug’.
Females are more active than males.

Symptoms
In milky stage, nymphs and adult suck sap from the grains. This cause incompletely filled panicles or panicles with empty grains. Bugs made the hole. Black and brown color appears around the holes and sooty mould may develop.

Management
Light traps are used to capture adult bugs.
Remove alternate host, Echinocloa from field.
Spray Malathion 50 EC @ 675 ml/ha
Use predators like tiger beetle.
4. Plant hopper
Order:- Hemiptera
Family:- Delphacidae
Two types of plant hopper are seen in rice field.

a) White Backed Plant Hopper (Sogatella furcifera)

Attacks in early crop season.
• Serious pest of high yielding varieties of paddy
• Cause rust red symptom

b) Brown Plant Hopper (Nilaparvata lugens)

Attacks in later crop season.
It transmit the ‘grassy stunt virus’ disease of rice.
Both white and brown plant hopper emit a toxin into the plant which produces a particular symptom known as ‘Hopper burn’.
It has a brown body and chestnut brown eyes.
Macropterous (long winged) and brachypterous (short winged) are the two forms of adult.

Management
Use multi gene resistant varieties: IR-36, IR-60
Alternate drying and wetting of field
20 * 15 cm spacing is suited. Avoid closer spacing.
Avoid use of insecticides causing resurgence such as synthetic pyrethroids methyl parathion etc.
Before use of insectides, drain the water and direct spray towards the base of the plants.
Spray of 100 ml Imidaeloprid (200 SL) with 250 liters of water.

Other pests of rice


a) Green leaf hopper (Jassidae)
It suck the sap from leaves and transmits ‘Tungro virus’.

b) Rice Thrips
Important pest of rice nursery

c) Rice gall midge
Cause “silver shoot and onion leaves” symptoms

d) Rice grass hopper
Damage by nibbling of rice ear head

MCQ


Q.No.1. The scientific name of Yellow Stem borer
Ans:- Scirphophaga incertulas

Q.No.2. The scientific name of White-backed Plant-hopper (WBPH)
Ans:- Sogatella furcifera

Q.NO.3. Which pest cause severe damage to rice panicle at night?
Ans:- Rice Army worm (Mythimna saparata)

Q.No.4. Chaffy grains with black spot is the infestation of
Ans:- Gundhi Bug (Leptocorisa acuta)

Q.No.5. Clipping off the top of rice seedlings containing immature stages of insects reduces the carry over of infestation of
Ans:- Rice hispa

Q.No.6. Absolute/Monophagus pest of rice is
Ans:- Rice stem borer

Q.No.7. Vector of rice 'Tungro' is
Ans:- Green Leaf Hopper

Q.No.8. Vector of rice 'Grassy stunt' disease is
Ans:- Brown Plant Hopper

Q.No.9. Gundhi bug cause damage on the stage of
Ans:- Milky stage

Q.No.10. 'Hopper burn' symptoms in rice due to
Ans:- Brown Plant Hopper

note:- "Hopper burn" is the common symptom of Nilaparvata lugens, "Nephotettix nigropictus" and "Sogatella furcifera".

Q.No.11. Which type of pyrethroids may cause resurgence of sucking pest in rice?
Ans:- Synthetic pyrethroids

Q.No.12. .......... act as a vector of ragged stunt and wilted stunt.
Ans:- Nilaparvata lugens

Q.No.13. ........... act as a vector for transitory yellowing diseases.
Ans:- Nilaparvata lugens

Q.No.14. Alternate wetting and drying is a good management technique for controlling ......
Ans:- Brown plant hopper

Q.No.15. Rice earhead bug belongs to ....... family.
Ans:- Alydidaea
